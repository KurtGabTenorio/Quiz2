My Neighbor Totoro


    It is a movie that focuses on two daughters & one father who moved out to a new home, for them to live closer to the daughters mother. One day, one of the daughters explore a forest to which she found a mysterious friendly animal spirit in the forest.

    Characters & Cast

    Tatsuo Kusakabe (Father), Shigesato Itoi. Mei Kusakabe ( Daughter with pink dress), Chika Sakamoto. Satsuki Kusakabe ( Daughter with yellow dress), Noriko Hidaka.

    Catbus, Naoki Tatsuta. Totoro (Grey Bunny), Hitoshi Takagi.
